Healing

Aftercare Tips For Your Ink

Proper aftercare is key to keeping your tattoo or piercing vibrant and healthy. Keep new tattoos clean and moisturized to prevent scabbing, fading, or distortion. Protective bandages simplify healing while letting you carry on with daily life, and a quality vegan moisturizer nourishes skin and preserves color during and after healing. Follow our expert tips to keep your ink bright and beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about our work? We're here to help — feel free to reach out.

What makes Tiger's Eye Tattoo stand out? +
We're focused on creating a comfortable, relaxing environment that rises far above the average shop experience. Our team brings over 20 years of combined experience and features award-winning artists ready to turn your ideas into art. We also provide expert piercings and use only high-quality materials, so every client leaves satisfied.
Where are you located? +
You'll find us downtown at 1200 Ember Street, Riverside — easy to reach for anyone searching for top-notch body art. Whether you want a custom tattoo, body piercing, or cosmetic work, we're dedicated to making your vision come to life. Call or visit for a free consultation.
What tattoo styles do you offer? +
Our artists specialize in a range of styles — bold traditional, intricate black and gray, fine line, color realism, portraits, and large-scale custom designs. If you're considering a cover-up or a larger project, a consultation is always recommended so we can match you with the right artist.
What should I look for in a piercing studio? +
Prioritize cleanliness, high-quality materials, and experienced piercers. A trusted shop will have strong reviews and a real commitment to safety. We pride ourselves on using the best products, expert piercers, and a relaxing environment. Stop by or contact us to learn more and book.
